The CPU (Central Processing Unit)

The Central Processing Unit(CPU) is known as the brains of the computer.
It is a critical part to a computer, but it can't not function without the
help of other components. The CPU is typically located in the center of the MotherBoard.

CPU Cooler

The CPU cooler's job is to provide cooling to the CPU
It is typically made with 1 fan or more and cooling fins and/or tubes. There is also water
cooling. These usaully use an All in one cooler(AIO). and require more space.
The CPU cooler sits on top on the cpu and requires thermal paste for bonding to the CPU. It is
also screwed into the MotherBoard itself.
